## 1. Two questions, always

A trade is screened on **two** independent axes — it must pass **both**:

1. **The instrument** — *how* you get exposure.
2. **The underlying** — *what* you're exposed to (business activity + financial ratios).

A halal company bought through a haram instrument (CFD, spread bet) is still **haram**.

---

## 2. Instrument rules (deterministic)

| Instrument | Ruling | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Spot ownership** of a halal asset (shares, physical/allocated commodity, spot crypto) | ✅ Permissible | Real ownership, immediate settlement (qabd) |
| **CFDs** | ❌ **Haram** | No real ownership of the underlying; synthetic; often overnight interest |
| **Futures / options** | ❌ Haram (majority view) | Deferred delivery & payment; gharar |
| **Spread betting** | ❌ Haram | Gambling economics (maysir) |
| **Margin / leverage / short-selling borrow** | ❌ Haram | Riba on the loan; selling what you don't own |

> **TradingView `TVC:GOLD`, CFD tickers, perpetual/futures — chart them for the read, but they are NOT the investable vehicle.** Express the idea through spot ownership.

## 3. Gold & silver (IFG)

Gold and silver are *ribawi* — they must be traded **spot**, **fully allocated**, with **immediate possession**. No deferment, no leverage, no paper/unallocated claims.

- ❌ **Avoid:** CFDs, spread bets, futures/options, ETFs holding gold *futures* (e.g. **DBP**, **DGL**), any unallocated "paper gold".
- ✅ **Use a physically-backed, allocated gold ETC**, or buy physical (BullionVault, Minted).

**Acceptable physically-backed gold ETCs (from IFG):**

| Region | Tickers |
|---|---|
| **UK / LSE** | **SGLN** (iShares Physical Gold), **SGLP** (Invesco Physical Gold), **PHGP** (WisdomTree Physical Gold), **RMAP** (Royal Mint Physical Gold), **XGLD** (Xtrackers Physical Gold), **SGBX/OGZU** (WisdomTree Physical Swiss Gold), **GBSS** (Gold Bullion Securities) |
| **US** | **IAU** (iShares Gold Trust), **SGOL** (abrdn Physical Gold), **GLDM** (SPDR Gold MiniShares), **BAR** (GraniteShares Gold), **GLD** (SPDR Gold Trust), **AAAU** (Perth Mint), **OUNZ** (VanEck Merk — delivery option) |

> The Zero "gold spot accumulation" thesis (sweep ~4000 + tap demand) is **valid via a physical ETC** — buy SGLN/SGLP/PHGP (UK) or IAU/SGOL/GLDM (US) in the discount, not the CFD.

## 4. Underlying screen (stocks) — AAOIFI-style

1. **Business activity** must be halal — no alcohol, pork, gambling, conventional finance/interest-lending, adult, tobacco, weapons (and for the cautious, material defence/military revenue).
2. **Financial ratios** (common thresholds):
   - Interest-bearing **debt / market cap < ~33%** (AAOIFI ~30%).
   - **Non-compliant / interest income < 5%** of revenue.
   - Purify any small impermissible dividend portion.
